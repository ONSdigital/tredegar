Local Statistics

Local statistics available to you

Official Labour Market Statistics (nomis)

Free access to detailed and up to date UK Labour Market statistics from official sources.

nomis allows you to:

  • design and define your own analysis for a wide range of statistical geographies
  • build your own geographic areas and compare with others areas
  • produce summary reports for individual areas
  • produce detailed analysis for multiple geographic areas

View more

Neighbourhood Statistics (NeSS)

Free access to a summary of your neighbourhood and small area data covering: the census, deprivation, health, local economy, education, housing and crime.

NeSS allows you to:

  • analyse and compare selected areas using maps and charts
  • pick and mix data from different datasets to compare different areas
  • view or download whole datasets

View more

2011 Census Data Explorer (Beta)

A tool providing free access to 2011 Census data including: demography, ethnicity and identity, health, housing and qualifications.

Data explorer allows you to:

  • find data by area or topic
  • customise online and download just the data you need
  • generate and download charts and maps for your data
